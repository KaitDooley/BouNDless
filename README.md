# Boundless

Boundless is a centralized coordination platform designed to support food security in the South Bend area. The application connects local food banks with community donors to ensure that resources are distributed efficiently and where they are needed most.

## Project Purpose
The goal of Boundless is to provide a clear, reliable link between those who want to help and the organizations on the front lines of food distribution. By providing up-to-date information on current needs, the platform helps donors make a more meaningful impact with their contributions.

## Key Features
* **Live Community Needs:** A direct view of what local food banks require at any given moment to prevent overstocking of some items and shortages of others.
* **Community Leaderboard:** A display of the top contributors to recognize the individuals and businesses making a difference in the area.
* **Specialized User Portals:** Tailored interfaces for both individual supporters and organizational partners to manage their interactions with the network.
* **South Bend Focus:** Specifically designed to address the unique logistical challenges of the local community.

## Core Technologies
The platform is built using a modern, reliable framework to ensure a fast and stable experience:
* **Next.js and TypeScript:** Used to create the visual interface and handle the application logic.
* **Firebase:** Used for secure account management and database storage.
* **Vercel:** Used to host the platform and ensure it is accessible to users at all times.
